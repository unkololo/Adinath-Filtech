  • SPUN X SPUN Yarn + Twill Weave - Combination - Suitable for Clear filtrate ,high tearing strength and Fine filtration Where Liquid is important.
  • Multi x Multi Yarn With Twill / Matty Weave Combination – Suitable for EASY Cake discharge , light Weight to Handel and where Solid cake is important , Fast Rate of Filtration as compared to Spun yarn type cloth
  • Mono x Multi Yarn with Satin weave – Highly Recommended for EASY Cake discharge ,fiber /lint free cloth , effective cake discharge of high sticky material , Faster filtration Rate ,less water consumption for cloth washing. Highly Recommended for STP and pharma applications. These type of cloth also provide the YIELD Increase.
  • Multi x Spun with Satin / Broken Twill Weave – Suitable for fine filtration in dyes and pigment industry and other ,high pressure filtration like Membrane press ,vary less or Nill Elonation so that Air permeability remains same till life .
